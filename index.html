Šiauliai, Lithuania

Šiauliai is the fourth-largest city in Lithuania, with a population of approximately 150,000 people. Its name translates to “Sun City,” and it is known for its long, sunny summer days. The city is rich in history and home to the Hill of Crosses, a powerful symbol of faith and resistance during periods of Russian occupation.
